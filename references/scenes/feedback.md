# 反馈与批评场景表达

## 1. 给负面反馈（委婉但清晰）

### 核心原则

先说正面，再说需要改进的地方，再说相信对方能改进。不要只说问题不说态度。

### 标准结构

```
"我注意到[行为/现象]。这是我的观察[具体描述]。我觉得如果[改进建议]会更好。"

或：

"I've been noticing [行为]. Here's what I'm observing: [具体描述]. I think if we adjusted [具体点], it could really level up [效果]. What do you think?"
```

### 真实场景举例

```
❌ "You always show up late to meetings."
✅ "I've noticed that the last few team meetings started a bit behind schedule. I know things get busy — just wanted to flag it because it makes it tricky to keep on time for everything that follows. Any thoughts on how to make this work better?"

❌ "Your reports are always late."
✅ "I've seen a few cases where the reports came in after the deadline, which made it hard for the team to stay on schedule. Would it help to set earlier internal deadlines, or is there something I can do to make the process easier?"

❌ "You don't communicate well."
✅ "There were a couple of times this week where I felt out of the loop on [具体事情]. I think it would be really helpful if we could sync a bit more frequently — maybe a quick async update once a day? Would that work for you?"

❌ "You're too quiet in meetings."
✅ "I've noticed you tend to hold back in group discussions. I really value your perspective — I'd love to hear your thoughts more often, even if it's just a quick note afterward."
```

## 2. 建设性反馈

```
"One thing I think could be really impactful is [建议]. The reason I say this is [原因]."

"I've been thinking about how you approach [事情], and I had an idea that might help: [建议]. Curious what you think."

"One area where I think you could really level up is [领域]. If you wanted, I can share some resources or walk through what worked for me."

"I really appreciate how you [做得好的地方]. One thing that could make it even stronger is [具体建议]."
```

## 3. 接受批评

```
"Thank you for sharing that — I hadn't thought about it from that angle. I appreciate the directness."

"Fair point. I can see why that came across that way. I'll work on [改进方向]."

"That's useful feedback. Let me reflect on it — would you be open to circling back next week to see how I'm doing with this?"

"I hear you. I think what you're pointing out is [你的理解]. I'm going to make a conscious effort to [改进]."
```

## 4. 表扬与鼓励

```
"I really wanted to call out [具体成就]. The way you [具体行为] was exactly what the moment needed."

"I thought you handled [情况] really well. A lot of people would have [可能的反应], but you [具体做法]. That made a real difference."

"Just wanted to say — I've noticed the work you've been putting in on [项目]. It shows, and the team really appreciates it."

"I was really impressed with [具体事情]. [具体细节] stood out to me. Keep up the great work!"
```

## 5. 帮别人改进（辅导式反馈）

```
"How do you think [某个方面] went? I have some thoughts, but I'm curious what your take is."

"Here's something I noticed: [观察]. Have you considered [替代方案]?"

"One thing I've tried that helped me with [类似挑战] was [做法]. Might be worth a shot if you're open to it."

"I have a suggestion — feel free to ignore if it doesn't resonate. But have you thought about [建议]?"
```

## 6. 反馈常用词汇

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Call out | 指出（问题） | I wanted to call this out early. |
| Flag | 标记/提出 | Just flagging a potential issue. |
| Nudge | 温和提醒 | Just a gentle nudge — no rush. |
| Level up | 提升 | This could really level up your work. |
| Constructive feedback | 建设性反馈 | I wanted to share some constructive feedback. |
| Direct | 直接坦诚 | I appreciate your directness. |
| Take it on board | 听进去 | I'll take that on board. |
| Honest assessment | 坦诚评估 | In my honest assessment... |
| Pull someone aside | 私下谈话 | Let me pull you aside for a quick word. |
| Coach | 辅导 | I'm here to coach, not just manage. |
