# 商务拓展（BD）岗位术语

## 合作与拓展

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| BD (Business Development) | 商务拓展 | BD is exploring partnerships in the Southeast Asia market. |
| Partnership | 合作关系 | We're exploring a strategic partnership with [公司]. |
| Strategic partnership | 战略合作 | A strategic partnership could unlock significant reach. |
| Co-branding | 联合品牌 | The co-branding campaign performed really well. |
| Cross-promotion | 互相推广 | We did a cross-promotion with a complementary brand. |
| Channel partner | 渠道合作伙伴 | Channel partners account for 40% of our sales. |
| Distribution partner | 分销合作伙伴 | We're looking for new distribution partners. |
| Affiliate | 联盟/附属 | The affiliate program brought in a lot of new users. |
| Integration | 整合合作 | The API integration took three months to complete. |
| White-label | 白牌/贴牌 | The product is available as a white-label solution. |

## 商务条款

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Deal | 交易/合作 | We closed the deal with [客户]. |
| Term sheet | 投资条款书 | They sent over a term sheet for the partnership. |
| MOU (Memorandum of Understanding) | 谅解备忘录 | We signed an MOU before moving to the full contract. |
| NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) | 保密协议 | We need to sign an NDA before sharing financials. |
| Pilot | 试点合作 | Let's start with a 3-month pilot to test the waters. |
| Trial period | 试用期 | The trial period went well — they want to move forward. |
| Contract terms | 合同条款 | The contract terms are still being negotiated. |
| Revenue share | 收入分成 | The revenue share model is 70/30 in our favor. |
| Commission | 佣金 | They earn a 10% commission on every deal. |
| Exclusive | 独家合作 | They want an exclusive deal for the region. |
| Non-compete | 竞业条款 | The non-compete clause is standard for this type of deal. |
| Renewal | 续约 | The contract renewal is coming up next quarter. |

## 客户关系

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Pipeline | 商机管道 | Our BD pipeline has ¥50M in potential deals. |
| Prospect | 潜在客户 | We have five hot prospects in the pipeline. |
| Lead | 销售线索 | We generated 200 leads from the conference. |
| Account | 客户账户 | This is a strategic account for us. |
| Key account | 大客户 | We have a dedicated team for key accounts. |
| Win rate | 赢单率 | Our win rate is around 35% for this segment. |
| Close rate | 成交率 | The close rate improved after we refined the pitch. |
| Conversion | 转化 | The conversion from trial to paid is at 60%. |
| Upsell | 追加销售 | We're upselling them on the premium tier. |
| Cross-sell | 交叉销售 | We're cross-selling the analytics add-on. |
| Churn | 客户流失 | We had two enterprise accounts churn this quarter. |
| Account health | 账户健康度 | The account health score is above 80. |
| Executive sponsor | 高层支持者 | Every key account needs an executive sponsor internally. |

## 商务沟通

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Pitch | 商业提案 | The pitch deck is ready for the client meeting. |
| Deck | 演示文稿 | Can you send me the latest version of the deck? |
| Outbound | 主动拓展 | Outbound efforts haven't been very effective. |
| Inbound | 被动获客 | Most of our deals come from inbound leads. |
| Cold call | 陌生电话 | Cold calling is still part of the BD playbook. |
| Warm introduction | 熟人引荐 | A warm introduction is much more effective than cold outreach. |
| Elevator pitch | 电梯演讲 | Practice your elevator pitch — you never know when it'll come up. |
| Gatekeeper | 把关人 | The executive assistant is the gatekeeper — we need to get past them. |
| Decision-maker | 决策人 | Who is the actual decision-maker on this deal? |
| Stakeholder map | 干系人图谱 | We mapped out all the stakeholders involved. |
