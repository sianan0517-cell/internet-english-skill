# 内容运营岗位术语

## 内容策略

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Content strategy | 内容策略 | Our content strategy targets three audience segments. |
| Content calendar | 内容日历 | Here's the content calendar for next month. |
| Editorial plan | 编辑计划 | The editorial plan covers blog, social, and email. |
| Content pillars | 内容支柱 | We organize our content around three pillars. |
| Thematic content | 主题内容 | We're doing a themed content series on sustainability. |
| Evergreen content | 常青内容 | Evergreen content drives consistent organic traffic. |
| Trending content | 热点内容 | Trending content gives us a quick traffic boost. |
| Series | 系列内容 | The series performed really well — we're doing a season 2. |
| Rerun / evergreen | 复投常青内容 | Let's rerun the top-performing posts this month. |
| Content audit | 内容审计 | We did a content audit to find underperforming pages. |

## 内容生产

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Copy | 文案 | The copy needs to be more conversational. |
| Draft | 草稿 | I've finished the first draft — can you review it? |
| Revision | 修订 | This is the third round of revisions. |
| Proofread | 校对 | Please proofread before we publish. |
| SEO copy | 搜索引擎优化文案 | We write SEO copy for all product pages. |
| Long-form content | 长内容 | Long-form content tends to rank better on Google. |
| Short-form content | 短内容 | Short-form content works better on social platforms. |
| UGC (User-Generated Content) | 用户生成内容 | We curate the best UGC for our community page. |
| PGC (Professional Generated Content) | 专业生成内容 | PGC gives us more control over brand voice. |
| Repurpose | 内容复用 | We repurpose blog posts into social media updates. |
| Syndicate | 内容分发 | We syndicate content to third-party platforms. |
| CMS (Content Management System) | 内容管理系统 | All content is managed through the CMS. |

## 数据与效果

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Engagement | 互动 | The engagement rate on this post is 8%. |
| Share of voice | 声量份额 | Our share of voice in the category is growing. |
| Reach | 触达 | The campaign reached 5 million people. |
| Impressions | 展示量 | We got 200K impressions from the newsletter. |
| Click-through rate (CTR) | 点击率 | The CTR on the email is 4.5%. |
| Open rate | 打开率 | The open rate for the weekly newsletter is 28%. |
| Bounce rate | 跳出率 | The bounce rate on the landing page is too high. |
| Dwell time | 停留时间 | Dwell time increased after we shortened the article. |
| Read-through rate | 读完率 | The read-through rate on the blog is 45%. |
| Conversion | 转化 | The content-led approach drove 30 conversions this month. |
| Traffic source | 流量来源 | Organic search is our biggest traffic source. |
| Referral traffic | 引荐流量 | Referral traffic from partners is growing. |
| Virality | 病毒传播系数 | The virality coefficient for this post was 1.3. |

## 平台与分发

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Platform strategy | 平台策略 | We have a different platform strategy for each channel. |
| Owned channel | 自有渠道 | Newsletter and app push are our owned channels. |
| Earned channel | 免费传播渠道 | Press coverage is our most credible earned channel. |
| Paid channel | 付费渠道 | We're increasing spend on paid channels this quarter. |
| Distribution | 分发 | We need a better distribution strategy for this content. |
| Cross-post | 多平台发布 | We cross-post to WeChat, Weibo, and Douyin. |
| Schedule | 排期发布 | We schedule all posts using the social media tool. |
| Peak posting time | 最佳发布时间 | Peak posting time for our audience is 8pm. |
| Feed algorithm | 信息流算法 | The feed algorithm change hurt our organic reach. |
| Hashtag strategy | 话题标签策略 | Let's rethink the hashtag strategy for this campaign. |
