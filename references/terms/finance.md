# 财务岗位术语

## 报表与指标

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| P&L (Profit and Loss) | 损益表 | Can you send me the P&L for Q3? |
| Revenue | 收入 | Revenue grew 30% YoY. |
| Gross revenue | 总收入 | Gross revenue before deductions is ¥100M. |
| Net revenue | 净收入 | Net revenue came in slightly below forecast. |
| EBITDA | 息税折旧摊销前利润 | EBITDA margin is at 25%, which is healthy. |
| Cost of goods sold (COGS) | 销售成本 | COGS increased due to higher supplier costs. |
| Gross margin | 毛利率 | Our gross margin is 68% — above industry average. |
| Operating expense (OpEx) | 运营费用 | OpEx is higher this quarter due to headcount growth. |
| SG&A (Selling, General & Admin) | 销售管理费用 | SG&A as a percentage of revenue needs to come down. |
| YoY (Year over Year) | 同比 | MAU is up 15% YoY. |
| QoQ (Quarter over Quarter) | 环比 | Revenue grew 8% QoQ. |
| MoM (Month over Month) | 环比（月度）| GMV dipped 3% MoM in August. |
| ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) | 年度经常性收入 | Our ARR crossed ¥50M this quarter. |
| MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) | 月度经常性收入 | MRR growth is the key metric for our model. |

## 预算与预测

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Budget | 预算 | We need to finalize the budget by end of month. |
| Forecast | 预测 | The Q4 forecast is looking conservative. |
| Variance | 差异 | There's a significant variance between plan and actuals. |
| Allocation | 分配 | The budget allocation for marketing increased. |
| Burn rate | 烧钱速度 | Our monthly burn rate is ¥5M. |
| Runway | 资金跑道 | We have 18 months of runway left. |
| Break-even | 盈亏平衡 | We're targeting break-even by Q4 next year. |
| Cash flow | 现金流 | Cash flow is positive for the first time. |
| Working capital | 营运资本 | We need to optimize our working capital. |
| CapEx (Capital Expenditure) | 资本支出 | CapEx this year is focused on infrastructure. |
| OpEx (Operating Expenditure) | 运营支出 | We're shifting from CapEx to OpEx for this initiative. |

## 投资与合规

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| ROI (Return on Investment) | 投资回报率 | The campaign had a 4x ROI. |
| IRR (Internal Rate of Return) | 内部收益率 | The IRR for this project is 22%. |
| NPV (Net Present Value) | 净现值 | The NPV is positive, so the investment makes sense. |
| Due diligence | 尽职调查 | We're in the due diligence phase of the acquisition. |
| Valuation | 估值 | The company is valued at 10x revenue. |
| Series A/B/C | 融资轮次 | They just closed a Series C at ¥200M. |
| Dilution | 股权稀释 | The new round will cause 15% dilution. |
| Equity | 股权 | She has 2% equity in the company. |
| Vesting | 期权归属 | The vesting schedule is 4 years with a 1-year cliff. |
| Audit | 审计 | The annual audit starts next month. |
| Compliance | 合规 | We're compliant with all regulatory requirements. |
| SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley) | 萨班斯法案 | SOX compliance is mandatory for public companies. |
