# 用户增长（UG / Growth）岗位术语

## 增长核心指标

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| MAU (Monthly Active Users) | 月活跃用户 | MAU grew 20% to 50M this quarter. |
| DAU (Daily Active Users) | 日活跃用户 | DAU is at 10M, and we're targeting 12M by year-end. |
| WAU (Weekly Active Users) | 周活跃用户 | WAU gives us a better read on engagement trends. |
| DAU/MAU ratio | 日活/月活比 | The DAU/MAU ratio is 0.25 — there's room to improve. |
| Stickiness | 用户粘性 | Stickiness improved after we added the daily check-in feature. |
| Retention rate | 留存率 | D1 retention is 60%, D7 drops to 30%. |
| Churn rate | 流失率 | Monthly churn rate is at 5% — we need to reduce it. |
| LTV (Lifetime Value) | 用户生命周期价值 | We need to increase LTV to justify the acquisition cost. |
| CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) | 获客成本 | Our CAC is ¥30, and LTV is ¥200 — a healthy ratio. |
| LTV/CAC ratio | 生命周期价值/获客成本比 | An LTV/CAC ratio above 3 is considered healthy. |
| Payback period | 回本周期 | The payback period for paid channels is 3 months. |

| Platform effect | 平台效应 | Platform effects are hard to replicate. |

| Paid growth | 付费增长 | Paid growth is scaling well but costs are rising. |
| Growth hacking | 增长黑客 | Growth hacking focuses on low-cost, high-impact tactics. |
