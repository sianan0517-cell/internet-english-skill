# 市场营销岗位术语

## 渠道与投放

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| SEM (Search Engine Marketing) | 搜索引擎营销 | SEM spend is up 20% this quarter. |
| SEO (Search Engine Optimization) | 搜索引擎优化 | SEO is a long-term play — we won't see results overnight. |
| CPC (Cost Per Click) | 单次点击成本 | Our average CPC went down after optimizing the ad copy. |
| CPM (Cost Per Mille) | 千次展示成本 | CPM varies significantly by placement and audience. |
| CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) | 单次获客成本 | Our target CPA is under ¥50 for this campaign. |
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | 点击率 | The CTR on the new banner is 3.2% — above industry average. |
| CVR (Conversion Rate) | 转化率 | CVR improved by 15% after the landing page redesign. |
| ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) | 广告支出回报率 | We're targeting a 5x ROAS on this campaign. |
| Impression | 展示量 | We hit 10M impressions in the first week. |
| Attribution | 归因 | Multi-touch attribution gives us a clearer picture. |
| Programmatic | 程序化投放 | We're moving more budget to programmatic buying. |
| Retargeting | 再营销 | Retargeting users who visited the page but didn't convert. |
| DSP (Demand-Side Platform) | 需求方平台 | We use a DSP to manage our programmatic campaigns. |

## 内容与传播

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Content marketing | 内容营销 | Content marketing builds trust over time. |
| Influencer marketing | 网红营销 | We're doing a campaign with three lifestyle influencers. |
| KOL (Key Opinion Leader) | 关键意见领袖 | We partnered with a tech KOL for the product launch. |
| UGC (User-Generated Content) | 用户生成内容 | UGC tends to feel more authentic than brand content. |
| PGC (Professional Generated Content) | 专业生成内容 | We balance PGC with UGC in our content strategy. |
| Viral | 病毒式传播 | The video went viral within 24 hours. |
| BGC (Brand-Generated Content) | 品牌生成内容 | BGC is important for brand awareness campaigns. |
| Organic reach | 自然触达 | Organic reach has dropped significantly on that platform. |
| Owned media | 自有媒体 | We should invest more in owned media channels. |
| Earned media | 免费媒体（口碑）| Earned media is hard to scale but very credible. |
| Pinned post | 置顶内容 | Let's pin the promotion post for the next 48 hours. |

## 活动与用户

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Campaign | 营销活动 | The campaign exceeded its targets by 30%. |
| Launch | 产品发布 | We're planning a coordinated launch across all channels. |
| Promo / promotion | 促销活动 | The promo code brought in a lot of first-time buyers. |
| Giveaway | 抽奖/赠品活动 | The giveaway engagement was off the charts. |
| Landing page | 落地页 | The landing page needs a stronger CTA. |
| Funnel | 营销漏斗 | We're focusing on the bottom of the funnel this quarter. |
| Brand awareness | 品牌认知度 | Brand awareness is hard to measure but crucial. |
| Brand positioning | 品牌定位 | Our brand positioning is "affordable premium." |
| Target audience / persona | 目标受众 | Our primary persona is 25-35 urban professionals. |
| Cohort | 用户群组 | Let's look at the cohort that converted from the email campaign. |
| Churn | 用户流失 | Churn rate is a key metric for our subscription model. |
| LTV (Lifetime Value) | 用户生命周期价值 | LTV is the metric we need to optimize for long-term. |
| NPS (Net Promoter Score) | 净推荐值 | Our NPS improved from 42 to 58 after the update. |
