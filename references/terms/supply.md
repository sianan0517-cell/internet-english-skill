# 供应链岗位术语

## 采购与供应商

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Procurement | 采购 | Procurement costs are up 10% this quarter. |
| Supplier | 供应商 | We've been working with the same supplier for 5 years. |
| Vendor | 供应商/服务商 | We need to qualify a new vendor for this service. |
| Sourcing | 寻源采购 | Sourcing is looking at three alternative suppliers. |
| Vendor management | 供应商管理 | Better vendor management can reduce lead times. |
| Supplier qualification | 供应商资质审核 | Supplier qualification takes about 2 months. |
| RFP (Request for Proposal) | 招标邀请书 | We sent out an RFP to five potential vendors. |
| RFQ (Request for Quotation) | 报价邀请书 | The RFQ closes next Friday. |
| Shortlist | 候选名单 | Three vendors made the shortlist. |
| PO (Purchase Order) | 采购订单 | The PO was approved and sent to the supplier. |
| Supply contract | 供货合同 | The supply contract guarantees volume for 12 months. |
| Lead time | 交货周期 | Lead time for this component is 6 weeks. |
| MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) | 最小起订量 | The MOQ is 1,000 units — it's too high for us. |

## 库存与仓储

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Inventory | 库存 | Inventory levels are higher than usual this quarter. |
| Stock | 库存 | We're running low on stock for this SKU. |
| Stockout | 缺货 | We had two major stockouts last month. |
| Overstock | 库存积压 | Overstock ties up working capital. |
| Dead stock | 滞销库存 | Dead stock needs to be liquidated. |
| Reorder point | 再订货点 | The reorder point is calibrated based on lead time. |
| Safety stock | 安全库存 | We maintain 2 weeks of safety stock. |
| Stock rotation | 库存轮换 | FIFO ensures proper stock rotation. |
| FIFO (First In, First Out) | 先进先出 | We use FIFO for perishable goods. |
| Warehouse | 仓库 | The new warehouse doubles our storage capacity. |
| Fulfillment | 履约 | Fulfillment time improved by 20% after the redesign. |
| Pick and pack | 拣货打包 | Pick and pack operations are the bottleneck. |

## 物流与配送

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Logistics | 物流 | Logistics costs are a major component of COGS. |
| Last-mile delivery | 最后一公里配送 | Last-mile delivery is the most expensive part of the chain. |
| 3PL (Third-Party Logistics) | 第三方物流 | We use a 3PL partner for all domestic deliveries. |
| FTL (Full Truckload) | 整车运输 | FTL is more cost-effective for large volumes. |
| LTL (Less Than Truckload) | 零担运输 | LTL works well for smaller shipments. |
| Express delivery | 快递 | Express delivery costs more but gets items there faster. |
| Cross-docking | 越库配送 | Cross-docking reduces warehouse handling time. |
| Route optimization | 路线优化 | Route optimization cut delivery time by 15%. |
| Carrier | 承运商 | We negotiate rates with multiple carriers annually. |
| Freight | 运费 | Freight rates are up 20% this quarter. |
| Customs clearance | 报关清关 | Customs clearance took an extra 5 days. |
| Incoterms | 国际贸易术语 | We use FOB for most of our international orders. |
| Bill of lading (BOL) | 提单 | The bill of lading is required for customs. |

## 供应链管理与指标

| 术语 | 释义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Supply chain | 供应链 | We're optimizing the entire supply chain end-to-end. |
| SCM (Supply Chain Management) | 供应链管理 | SCM systems help track inventory in real time. |
| End-to-end | 端到端 | We need visibility across the end-to-end chain. |
| Demand forecasting | 需求预测 | Demand forecasting accuracy is at 85%. |
| Demand planning | 需求计划 | Demand planning uses historical data and trends. |
| Supply planning | 供应计划 | Supply planning needs to catch up with demand. |
| Bullwhip effect | 牛鞭效应 | The bullwhip effect causes over-ordering upstream. |
| Lean supply chain | 精益供应链 | We adopted lean principles to reduce waste. |
| Just-in-time (JIT) | 准时制 | Just-in-time reduces inventory holding costs. |
| Lead time variability | 交货周期波动 | Lead time variability makes forecasting harder. |
| Stock turnover | 库存周转率 | Stock turnover improved from 6 to 9 times per year. |
| Fill rate | 订单满足率 | Our fill rate is 98% — industry leading. |
| Order accuracy | 订单准确率 | Order accuracy is at 99.5%. |
| OTIF (On-Time In-Full) | 按时足量交付率 | OTIF is a key vendor performance metric. |
| Disruption | 供应链中断 | The port closure caused a major supply chain disruption. |
| Single point of failure | 单点故障 | Our supply chain has too many single points of failure. |
